About Us

Nrityaswara Kalaangan School of Dance, founded and directed by Mrs. Gauri Taval, is a reputed cultural institution in Pune dedicated to nurturing young talents and preserving the timeless art of Bharatanatyam. Situated at Nanded City, Sinhgad Road, the institute goes beyond teaching dance as movement—it instills discipline, creativity, and cultural values, making it a true hub for tradition and artistic expression.

The school’s mission is to promote, preserve, and spread Indian Classical Dance, particularly Bharatanatyam, both nationally and internationally. Its vision is not only to train dancers in technical skills but also to shape them into complete performers who embody grace, discipline, and devotion. Under the expert guidance of Mrs. Gauri Taval, an accomplished dancer with more than 15 years of experience, students learn intricate footwork, mudras, abhinaya, and stage presentation. With an M.A. in Bharatanatyam and accolades such as the Best Teacher Award, Nritya Vibhushan Award, Maharashtra Kala Sanman Award, Nrityasadhana Award, and Excellence Award, Mrs. Taval brings both passion and prestige to the institute.
